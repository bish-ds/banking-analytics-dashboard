Key Insights

💰

Deposits & Savings Correlation

High correlation between Bank Deposits and Saving Accounts indicates overlapping financial behavior — customers who actively deposit also grow savings proportionally.

📈

Income-Age Lifecycle

Moderate correlations of Age and Income with balances reflect common lifecycle trends — higher earners and older individuals accumulate more across all account types.

🏠

Properties Independence

Property ownership shows low correlation with banking variables, driven by external factors like location, market conditions, and inheritance rather than banking activity.

🏢

Business vs Personal

Business Lending is moderately linked to Bank Loans but uncorrelated with deposits, indicating it serves a distinct customer segment with different financial needs.
